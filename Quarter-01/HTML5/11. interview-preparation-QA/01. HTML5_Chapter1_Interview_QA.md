# HTML5 -- Chapter 1 Industrial Interview Questions (README)

## 1. What is the purpose of the `<!DOCTYPE html>` declaration in HTML5?

**Answer:**\
`<!DOCTYPE html>` tells the browser to render the document in
**standards mode** rather than quirks mode. Unlike older versions, HTML5
uses a short, simple doctype for universal compatibility.\
Standards mode ensures your layout, CSS, and JavaScript behave
consistently across browsers.

**Practical Tip:**\
Open DevTools → Console → check for "Quirks Mode" warnings when
debugging layout issues.

**Difficulty:** Beginner

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 2. Explain the role of the `<html>` tag and its `lang` attribute.

**Answer:**\
`<html>` is the root element, and `lang="en"` improves SEO,
accessibility, and screen‑reader pronunciation.
